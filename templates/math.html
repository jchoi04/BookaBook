Precalculus: Mathematics for Calculus, 6th Student Edition; James Stewart, Lothar Redlin, Salee Watson
Problem solving and mathematical modeling are introduced early and reinforced throughout, providing students with a solid foundation in the principles of mathematical thinking. Comprehensive and evenly paced, the book provides complete coverage of the function concept, and integrates a significant amount of graphing calculator material to help students develop insight into mathematical ideas.
Essential Calculus, Second Edition; James Stewart
A concise approach to teaching calculus that focuses on major concepts, and supports those concepts with precise definitions, patient explanations, and carefully graded problems. Despite the more compact size, the book has a modern flavor, covering technology and incorporating material to promote conceptual understanding.
Calculus 2; Tunc Geveci
The distinguishing features of the book are the focus on the concepts, essential functions and formulas of calculus and the effective use of graphics as an integral part of the exposition. Formulas that are not significant and exercises that involve artificial algebraic difficulties are avoided. Calculus II covers techniques and applications of integration, improper integrals, infinite series, linear and separable first-order differential equations, parametrized curves and polar coordinates.
Linear Algebra and Its Applications; David C. Lay, Steven R. Lay, Judi J. McDonald
Traditional linear algebra texts start with familiar, concrete material, making the early stages of the course easy for students. However, when abstract concepts like linear independence, spanning, subspace, vector space, and linear transformations are introduced, students often struggle. Instructors agree that these concepts are fundamental but challenging. This text addresses the issue by introducing these concepts early in a familiar Rn setting, developing them gradually, and revisiting them throughout the text. This approach ensures that when discussed in the abstract, students can readily understand these essential concepts.
